Aries

Element

Fire

Symbol

Ram

Modality

Cardinal

Song Rec

Lash Out - Alice Merton

overview

Aries is the first sign of the zodiac. Aries suns generally know what they want and they know how to get it, but they rarely depend upon underhanded or dishonest methods to achieve their goals./p>

strengths

Aries suns are natural athletes and like to use their bodies to get things done. Aries is an independent and innovative sign, and they often enjoy a challenge.

weaknesses

Aries suns are prone to ignoring the emotions of themselves and others. They also tend to focus on what's right in front of them, and they also tend to not plan ahead. Impatience is a vice for Aries suns.

overview

Taurus is the second sign of the zodiac. Taurus suns have a dependable, calming presence. They are sensual in nature and place importance on security within their life.

strengths

Taurus suns are very hard workings and are also skilled at seperating work from leisure. This is a reliable sign, and they are very loyal to the people they care about.

weaknesses

Taureans value their personal belongings and sometimes come off as possessive. They often are deeply rooted in their beliefs, which leads to this sign being very stubborn.

Song Rec

Steady - Hotel Fiction

overview

Gemini is the third sign in the zodiac. Gemini suns are flexible and adaptable. They are often easily able to adopt the mood of those around them.

strengths

Gemini is a social sign, and they are excellent at fitting in with others. They are not easily indimidated and enjoy intellectual conversations.

weaknesses

Gemini suns often are curious to a fault and are prone to gossiping. Although they are social and have many friends, intimacy is difficult for this sign. Geminis may also struggle to take things seriously.

Symbol

Crab

Modality

Cardinal

Song Rec

Retrospect - Vistas

overview

Cancer is the fourth sign in the zodiac. This is a highly empathetic sign, as well as very emotional. Cancers tend to withdraw or retreat when they feel hurt.

strengths

Cancers are very protective of themselves and their loved ones. They have big imaginations and like to surround themeselves with objects and people that intrigue them.

weaknesses

Cancers tend to be moody, and often are resistent to change. They can sometimes be manipulative and may struggle with identifying the source of their emotions - they may take their emotions out on other people.

Girl is a Queen - Splash

overview

Leo is the fifth sign of the zodiac. Leo suns have big dreams and love to make people happy. They appreciate the good things in life and are willing to work to attain them.

strengths

Leos are very hardworking and confident, and they take pride in themselves and their work. They are extremely tenacious and rarely give up on things they care about.

Leos' confidence tends to come across as conceited and entitled. Their perserverence also makes them prone to holding grudges.

broken wheel - Isaac Gracie

overview

Virgo is the sixth sign of the zodiac. Virgos are organized, hardworking, and love to learn. Virgo suns value feeling appreciated and and useful.

strengths

Virgo suns are self-sufficient and responsible. They are very thorough and are very proud of their accomplishments. Virgos are very loyal and almost always willing use their knowledge to help others.

weaknesses

Virgos tend to be embarassed easily and take longer than many other signs to open up around others. They also tend to be perfectionists and sometimes leave projects unfinished out of fear that they won't turn out how they want.

overview

Libra is the seventh sign of the zodiac. This sign is sociable and intellectual, and they enjoy collaborating with others.

overview

Scorpio is the eighth sign of the zodiac. Scorpio suns are known for their intensity and determination. They aren't scared of the darker sides of life. Scorpios are almost always willing to investigate what other signs often aren't.

Scorpio suns are not indimidated by anything or anyone. This is an intellectual sign that makes a great leader, as they are trust-worthy and and loyal.
