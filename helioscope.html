Overview

So what is a sun sign?

Your sun sign is what directs the rest of your star chart. It represents your conscious mind, your will, and your creativity. Our life purpose is derived from our sun signs, as well as our egos.

Your sun is your basic identity.

The sun moves into a sign of the zodiac every month, so your sun sign is determined by the month and day you were born.

Elements

The twelve signs are divided into four elements - fire, water, earth, and air - which all have a unique way of expressing their energies The elements balance eachother out, distributing strengths and weaknesses throughout the zodiac.

Fire - Aries, Leo, Sagittarius

Fire signs are always very exciting to be around. They are strong-willed, passionate, and reactive, and can come across as aggressive at times. They are brave and energetic, which leads to impulsive tendencies.

Water - Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces

Water signs are empathetic, intuitive and very sensitive. They feel more on an emotional level than other signs, and can easily pick up on the emotions of others.

Earth - Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn

Earth signs are very practical, realistic, pragmatic, and grounded. They tend to make decisions based on facts and do not allow themselves to be swept up in their emotions or passion.

Air - Gemini, Libra, Aquarius

Air signs are often over-thinkers, but they are also very social, and are known to love connecting with others. They often make decisions based on logic and intellect, and because of this they often come across as emotionless.

Modalities

Modalities further categorize the twelve zodiac signs, and they represent the sign's ability to move and evolve with time.

There are three modalities - cardinal, mutable, and fixed.

Cardinal - Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn

The cardinal modality represents beginnings, action, and initiation. These signs may tend to be more traditional, and come across as cautious. However, they are trusted mediators and are known to be centered and reasonable.

Mutable - Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces

The mutable modality represents endings and change. These signs are adaptable and versatile, but also can be unpredicable and inconsistent. They have many interests and require a lot of stimulation and change to be happy.

Fixed - Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius

The fixed modality represents sustainability. These signs tend to have a strong and forceful energy, but also stagnant and unforgiving. Fixed signs tend to like routine and prefer to do things their way.
